Lightning-fast access to the internet and the modern world

Discover very high speed internet and enjoy an unparalleled speed of information transmission with optical fiber. Downloading a film via a legal video-on-demand offer only requires a few seconds with very high-speed optical fiber compared to several minutes with an ADSL connection. Downloading, sharing large files, simultaneous applications… Perform an eligibility test to find out your potential throughput!

Immerse yourself in the world of very high speed!

The capacity of optical fiber to transport very high speeds offers the possibility for different people in the same household to make simultaneous uses without constraint, thanks to ultra-fast speeds ranging from 100 Mb / s to 1 Gb / s! .

Professional performance at home

Choosing optical fiber means opting for a very high speed reliable and efficient internet. The symmetrical speeds available by fiber thus allow the development of new applications such as teleworking, telemedicine or even home automation. .

All your applications simultaneously

Connect all at the same time, without slowing down or cutting out! With the power of FTTH fiber, experience fiber fully: simultaneous connections on different screens (including 4K and Ultra HD), network games, direct access services, cloud file management… everything goes faster!.

Very high speed for businesses

Because eligibility for optical fiber and access to a wide choice of offers is a determining factor in the choice of setting up a business in a territory, Covage deploys networks open to all service operators, local, national or international and allows them to support companies in their digital transformation. .

A high-performance network and guaranteed service

The optical fiber offers a guaranteed symmetrical speed and almost unlimited, without any constraint of distance, and an availability rate close to 100%. Combined with centralized network supervision, local technical teams and guaranteed recovery time (GTR), the advantages of fiber make it the most reliable and long-lasting solution for businesses. .
